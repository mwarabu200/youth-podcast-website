Why This Podcast Exists

My podcast is about sharing authentic stories, creating awareness, and promoting dialogue on topics that impact youth, spirituality, and community growth.

"When It Hurts" is a podcast dedicated to exploring the tough conversations that often go unheard. We dive into raw, real stories about personal struggles, challenges, and how to navigate through life's difficult moments.

Each episode brings you unfiltered conversations with guests who share their journey through pain, healing, and growth. From mental health battles to overcoming setbacks in career and relationships, we tackle it all.

This podcast is for anyone who has ever felt lost, hurt, or uncertain. We’re here to remind you that you’re not alone. "When It Hurts" is a space for healing, understanding, and finding strength in vulnerability.

If you’re ready to embrace the hurt and learn how to rise stronger, "When It Hurts" is the podcast for you. Tune in, listen to the stories, and let’s heal together.
