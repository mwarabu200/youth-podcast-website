Why This Podcast Exists

"When It Hurts" is a podcast about sharing authentic stories, creating awareness, and promoting dialogue on topics that impact youth, spirituality, and community growth.

We explore the tough conversations that often go unheard. Through raw, real stories, we dive into personal struggles, healing, and growth.

Each episode brings unfiltered conversations with guests who’ve faced challenges in mental health, careers, relationships, and more—showing how they overcame adversity and grew stronger.

This podcast is for anyone who has ever felt lost, hurt, or uncertain. We're here to remind you: you are not alone. "When It Hurts" is your space for healing, understanding, and finding strength in vulnerability.

Ready to grow through what you go through? Tune in and be part of the healing journey.
